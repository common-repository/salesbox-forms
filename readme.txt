== Description ==

Salesbox – The Best CRM for WordPress and SMEs

Begin catching the hot website visitors with Salesbox lead generation forms for WordPress. Salesbox CRM for WordPress and SMEs lets you choose to add any standard data field or any custom field from contacts, companies and prospects to your Contact 7 forms by Salesbox.

Salesbox CRM for WordPress and SMEs also allows you to add dropdown to let your visitor choose what they are interested in so it’s clear when you begin following up your new leads. Salesbox CRM for SMEs adds the new leads directly to the collaboration section called Unassigned and pings your users in real time that a new lead has arrived.

Allow your users to assign the new leads to themselves or choose to distribute the leads to the proper person.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin from Wordpress plugin directory or upload the entire `salesbox-crm-form` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** screen (**Plugins > Installed Plugins**).
3. You will find **Salesbox forms** menu in your WordPress admin screen. Go to **Salesbox forms -> Connect to Salesbox**.
4. Click **Connect to Salesbox CRM**, you will be redirected to Salesbox's login page, enter the credentials there, then you'll be redirected back to the plugin.
5. Now the plugin is connected to your Salesbox account, add new forms and contacts will flow to Salesbox!

*For this plugin to work you need to subscribe to Salesbox and also the Wordpress forms plugin by Salesbox from Salesbox billing page.*
